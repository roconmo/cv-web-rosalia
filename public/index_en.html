About Me

A Technical Background in Service of Organisational Transformation

I have over 20 years in complex technology environments: data analysis, cloud, transformation projects. That experience taught me to read organisations from within: how information flows, where bottlenecks concentrate, what prevents teams from coordinating.

I now apply that perspective to help organisations understand how they really work, identify where to improve, and design viable initiatives. My advantage: I understand both languages — business and technology — and know how to translate between them.

I am currently working on a process diagnosis for a family-owned construction materials distribution company, focusing on cross-department coordination and improvement proposals for management.

Services

I work with executives who know something isn't working well internally, but aren't sure exactly where or how to tackle it.

Organisational Diagnosis

I analyse how work actually flows between departments to identify bottlenecks, critical dependencies and improvement opportunities that aren't always visible from the inside.

Direct observation, team interviews, process mapping and friction point analysis.

Process Improvement

I design initiatives aimed at simplifying processes, improving cross-team coordination and making day-to-day execution easier.

Concrete, prioritised proposals adapted to what the team can actually implement.

Project Management

I turn the diagnosis into an action plan with clear phases, owners and metrics, and support implementation so that changes stick.

When the project involves technology, I act as a bridge between technical and business teams so that the implementation doesn't get lost in translation.

What You'll Get

I don't just deliver a report. At the end of the diagnosis you'll have:

  • a clear picture of how work actually flows;
  • the main bottlenecks identified;
  • initiatives prioritised by impact and effort;
  • a roadmap for implementing improvements.
