Projects

Customer Experience and Process Optimisation — B2B and Retail Distribution

Consulting project to analyse and improve the customer experience and the functioning of the commercial and operational processes at a family-owned distribution company specialising in construction materials, bathrooms and ceramics with over 80 years of history.

The project began with a specific focus on customer service, but direct observation revealed that a large part of that experience depends on how information flows, on coordination between departments, and on the clarity of internal processes.

The work includes observation in shops, showrooms, warehouses and the Call Centre; interviews with employees and managers; process analysis; identification of manual tasks, bottlenecks and key-person dependencies; and design of improvement proposals, pilots and metrics to assess their impact.

Approach: research first, understand how work is actually done, then design practical solutions that improve both the customer experience and internal operations.
